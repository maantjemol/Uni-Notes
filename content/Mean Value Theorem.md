# Mean Value Theorem
## Definition 
1. Take a continuous [[functions]] between $[a, b]$
2. let $f$ be differentable on (a,b) then there exists a C (a,b) such that:
$$f'(c)=\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}$$
$$f(b)=f(a)+f'(c)(b-a)$$
